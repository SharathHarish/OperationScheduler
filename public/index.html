Welcome to Artemis Hospital

Best Super Speciality Hospital & Trauma Centre in Gurgaon

Established in 2007, Artemis Hospitals was founded to provide the highest standards of medical care to patients from Delhi-NCR, other states in India, and abroad. We provide access to a multitude of in-patient and out-patient services across 40+ specialties with a team of over 400 doctors and 12 Centres of Excellence, including cardiology, oncology, gastroenterology, internal medicine, nephrology, and orthopaedics.

Upholding the highest standards of quality care and patient safety in tertiary healthcare services, Artemis Hospitals was the first hospital in Gurgaon to receive accreditation from the Joint Commission International (JCI) and the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers (NABH). As one of the best hospitals for heart, kidney, and cancer care in Gurgaon, we provide state-of-the-art medical equipment and technologies to ensure precise treatments for patients.
